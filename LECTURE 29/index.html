Formula 1 Motor Sport

Formula 1: Drive to Survive

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international auto racing featuring single-seater cars. The 2025 season includes 24 Grand Prix races around the world, starting in Australia and ending in Abu Dhabi. Renowned for speed, strategy, and technology, F1 attracts millions of fans globally. It showcases teams and drivers competing with cutting-edge cars on iconic circuits, blending thrilling races with advanced engineering excellence. The season also includes sprint races, adding excitement and variety to the traditional race format.
